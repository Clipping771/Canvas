# 02 - User Personas

> **Product Axiom:** If we don't solve the teacher's problem first, the student will never see the product.

## 1. The Gatekeeper: Sarah, High School Physics Teacher
- **Context:** Sarah teaches 4 classes of Year 11 Physics. She has 120 students total.
- **The Friction:** She spends 15 hours a week grading paper exams and assignments. Creating visual diagrams for her lesson slides takes hours in PowerPoint. Her students constantly struggle to visualize magnetic fields in 3D.
- **The Vinci Board Solution:** Sarah uses Vinci Board to drag-and-drop a pre-built magnetic field simulation onto her lesson canvas. She distributes the canvas to her students. When students complete the embedded quiz by drawing vectors on the canvas, Vinci's Hybrid AI grades 85% of them automatically, leaving only 15% (nuanced/creative answers) for her to manually review.
- **Success Metric for Sarah:** Hours saved per week.

## 2. The End User: Leo, Year 11 Student
- **Context:** Leo is a visual learner who struggles with static textbook equations.
- **The Friction:** When he does his homework at 8 PM, Sarah isn't there to answer his questions. If he gets stuck on a vector addition problem, he just stops working.
- **The Vinci Board Solution:** Leo opens the canvas assignment. He drags vectors around to "feel" how they add up. When he gets stuck, he pings the AI Learning Partner. The AI doesn't give him the answer; it highlights his vector and asks a Socratic question based on his specific mistake.
- **Success Metric for Leo:** "Aha!" moments / reduced homework abandonment.

## 3. The Buyer: Mark, School IT Director
- **Context:** Mark manages software licenses for 2,000 students.
- **The Friction:** He hates fragmented tools. Teachers buying individual $15/month subscriptions is a nightmare for data privacy compliance (FERPA/GDPR).
- **The Vinci Board Solution:** Mark is pitched on a centralized Enterprise dashboard. Vinci Board integrates with Google Workspace SSO. Mark has full audit logs of what the AI is saying to students, ensuring safety compliance.
- **Success Metric for Mark:** Zero compliance breaches + single consolidated invoice.
